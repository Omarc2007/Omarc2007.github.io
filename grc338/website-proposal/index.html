Final Project Proposal

Introduction

Cole Café

Cole Café is a fun dessert café where people can enjoy fresh pastries, chocolates, sweet treats, and drinks. We make our desserts every day using quality ingredients and serve them in a warm and friendly place. Whether you want a quick snack, a dessert after dinner, or a place to meet with friends, Cole Café has something for everyone. Our goal is to make every visit simple, fun, and delicious.

Target Audience

Cole Café is for anyone who loves desserts. Families, students, friends, and people of all ages can stop by to enjoy a sweet treat.

The website will help customers look at the menu, learn about the café, find the location, see the hours, and order food online. It should be easy to use and full of colorful pictures that make people excited to visit.

Comparative Analysis

MochiMunch

mochimunch.toast.site

MochiMunch has a colorful website with large pictures of every menu item. The food is grouped into sections, making it easy for customers to find what they want. The simple layout keeps the focus on the desserts.

See's Candies

sees.com

See's Candies uses neat rows of products with clear pictures, prices, and sales. The website is clean and easy to browse, making shopping simple. The organized layout helps customers compare different treats.

Cinnabon

cinnabon.com

Cinnabon uses large banners and bright colors to grab attention right away. The homepage highlights special menu items and makes online ordering easy. The design feels warm, welcoming, and exciting.
